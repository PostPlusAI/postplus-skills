# Surreal Escalation

Template reference: `wild_card`.

## Use When

Use when an ordinary product action can trigger a clearly fictional action, metaphor, or heightened world that expresses energy, intensity, flavor, or transformation.

## Grammar

- Begin with a simple ordinary product interaction.
- Use one decisive transition into the fictional world.
- Escalate through one coherent action or metaphor system.
- Reintroduce ingredient, texture, package, or product behavior during the climax.
- End with a readable product hero that resolves the spectacle.

## Camera, Edit, And Sound

- Contrast stable opening coverage with fast action framing, push-ins, impact cuts, or bounded slow motion.
- Use sound to mark transition, impact, ingredient motion, and final hero resolution.
- Keep the product visible at the trigger and ending even if absent during intermediate action.

## Proof And Continuity

- Label the spectacle as fictional metaphor. Use separate literal evidence if the brief needs a real claim.
- Lock protagonist, product package, action direction, environment, effect progression, and final product orientation.

## Avoid

- Copying exact characters, institutional settings, destruction beats, or recognizable action choreography.
- Presenting fictional strength, health, or performance as literal proof.
- Effects that hide or mutate the product hero.

## Generator Risks

Action continuity, destruction physics, debris, slow-motion particles, faces, product reappearance, ingredient motion, and package legibility.
