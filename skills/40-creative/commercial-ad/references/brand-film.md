# Brand-Film Narrative

Template reference: `tv_spot`.

## Use When

Use when the product belongs inside a compact human story with visible pressure, a tactile product hinge, a changed state, and an identity-style close.

## Grammar

- Establish a specific external pressure through behavior, environment, or one coherent visual device.
- Escalate the pressure while the product remains present or naturally enters the scene.
- Use one ordinary product gesture as the causal hinge.
- Include a sensory or functional detail at the moment of interaction.
- Show changed posture, pace, attention, or social connection after use.
- Resolve with a stable hero or post-produced end card.

## Camera, Edit, And Sound

- Use controlled location coverage, tracking when the person moves, and macro inserts for the product hinge.
- Let sound escalate with pressure, then simplify or change after product interaction.
- Reserve the strongest music resolution for the changed state or close.

## Proof And Continuity

- Treat the human state change as lifestyle evidence, not literal performance proof by itself.
- Lock persona, wardrobe, product state, recurring visual device, location progression, and light direction.

## Avoid

- Abstract lifestyle montage without a causal product gesture.
- Multiple unrelated pressure metaphors.
- Generated slogans, logos, or end-card typography.

## Generator Risks

Multi-scene continuity, composited visual devices, persona consistency, product visibility, crowd/background drift, and end-state causality.
