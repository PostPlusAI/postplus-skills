# CGI Product World

Template reference: `hypermotion_oj`.

## Use When

Use for beverages, food, beauty, ingredients, texture, freshness, scent, or material stories that benefit from controlled CGI motion.

## Grammar

- Begin with ingredient, material, liquid, or particle motion before the package reveal.
- Use one coherent motion family such as burst, spiral, stream, orbit, or float.
- Transition from raw elements to texture and then to the stable package hero.
- End with the product readable and the surrounding elements resolving around it.

## Camera, Edit, And Sound

- Use macro views, fly-throughs, tracked motion, rotation, and slow push-ins only when they reveal a property.
- Shift from fast build to slower hero resolution.
- Synchronize whooshes, splashes, impacts, and music accents to object motion.

## Proof And Continuity

- Classify the result as sensory visualization unless literal product facts support more.
- Lock package geometry, palette, ingredient set, liquid behavior, light direction, and final hero orientation.

## Avoid

- Random particles or ingredients unrelated to supplied facts.
- Constant motion after the product needs to become readable.
- Generated label copy or unsupported ingredient claims.

## Generator Risks

Fluid physics, particle continuity, food texture, condensation, reflections, readable package geometry, and effect occlusion.
