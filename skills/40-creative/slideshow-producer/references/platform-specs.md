# Platform Specifications

## TikTok Slideshow

### Canvas
- Dimensions: 1080 × 1920 px
- Aspect ratio: 9:16
- Orientation: vertical / portrait

### Safe Zones
- **Top ~60px**: caption overlay, account handle, following button. Avoid critical content here.
- **Bottom ~80px**: like bar, comment count, share button, music note. Avoid CTAs and text here.
- **Sweet spot**: between y=80 and y=1840

### Overlay Text
- Font: bold sans-serif (Helvetica Bold preferred)
- Size: 56px (relative to 1080px canvas width)
- Max 2 lines, 3-8 words per slide
- White (#FFFFFF) with black (#000000) stroke, 3px
- Position: upper-center (y ~100-500)
- Lowercase-heavy, conversational, no ending punctuation on single lines

### Slide Count
- Short: 3-4 slides
- Standard: 5-7 slides
- Long: 8-10 slides
- Default: 6 slides

### Export
- 1080p, not 4K (4K signals produced content to the algorithm)
- PNG format from generation, final export as JPEG or PNG
- Slight compression is fine — adds authenticity

## Instagram Carousel

### Canvas
- Dimensions: 1080 × 1350 px (4:5, preferred) or 1080 × 1080 px (1:1)
- Aspect ratio: 4:5 or 1:1

### Safe Zones
- Top ~50px for action bar
- Bottom ~50px for carousel dot indicator and action buttons

### Text Strategy
- Most text goes in the Instagram caption field, not overlaid on images
- Minimal overlay text: 0-5 words per slide
- Bold readable sans-serif, center or lower-center placement

### Slide Count
- 3-5 slides for quick stories
- 6-8 slides for detailed content
- 10 slides maximum

---

## Shared Rules (Both Platforms)

- No logos unless explicitly required
- No app store badges or browser chrome
- Safe zone dimensions are guidelines, not rigid crop lines
- Verify placement in platform preview before publishing
