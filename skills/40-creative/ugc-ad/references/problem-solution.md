# Problem And Solution

Template references: `ugc_gadget_saved_me`, `ugc_secret_hack_reveal`, `ugc_how_to`.

## Use When

Use when the product solves a visible routine friction, contains a demonstrable feature, follows a short process, or creates an observable output.

## Grammar

- Open on a concrete pressure, curiosity gap, or changed routine.
- Introduce the product as the next physical action, not as a detached announcement.
- Show the operation in causal order with one dominant action per beat.
- Hold long enough to see the output, texture, state change, or completed task.
- Close with the creator using, tasting, holding, or recommending the result.

## Camera And Sound

- Keep active controls, ingredients, hands, and output centered and unobstructed.
- Use static medium shots for explanation and close inserts for operation.
- Capture clicks, motors, pouring, tapping, brushing, or other useful proof sounds.

## Proof And Continuity

- Require a visible input-action-output chain.
- Lock product controls, ingredient or prop amounts, action order, output state, and room layout.

## Avoid

- Calling a feature secret when no distinct action or result is visible.
- Skipping from setup to result without mechanism evidence.
- Unsupported speed, power, health, or performance claims.

## Generator Risks

Control-panel text, hand placement, liquid behavior, appliance geometry, process continuity, and speech density.
