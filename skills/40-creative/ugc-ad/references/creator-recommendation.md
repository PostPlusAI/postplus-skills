# Creator Recommendation

Template references: `ugc`, `ugc_selfie_testimonial`, `ugc_direct_to_camera`, `product_review`.

## Use When

Use for products that benefit from a personal recommendation, clear feature explanation, comfort reaction, material review, or everyday use verdict.

## Grammar

- Open on a reaction, specific problem, arrival, or direct viewer challenge.
- Show the product near the creator's face or chest within 0-3 seconds.
- Alternate direct-address medium or close shots with one or two product inserts.
- Demonstrate one primary use, adjustment, control, texture, fit, or result.
- Return to the creator for an embodied verdict or use-case recommendation.

## Camera And Sound

- Prefer static or lightly handheld phone-adjacent framing.
- Use bright window light or warm practical indoor light.
- Let clear speech lead; keep music low and use practical sounds as punctuation.

## Proof And Continuity

- Pair every spoken benefit with a visible action, detail, or state change.
- Lock creator identity, wardrobe, product geometry, room, light direction, and product state.

## Avoid

- Generic excitement without evidence.
- Feature lists spoken over an unrelated beauty shot.
- More than one primary product promise in a short clip.

## Generator Risks

Hands near the lens, small controls or screens, product labels, synchronized speech, mirror reflections, and consistent product scale.
