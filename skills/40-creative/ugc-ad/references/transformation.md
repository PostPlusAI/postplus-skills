# Transformation

Template references: `ugc_before_and_after`, `mess_to_fresh`.

## Use When

Use when the product creates a visible cleanup, grooming, repair, organization, or other state change that can be shown credibly.

## Grammar

- Open on the problem surface or incorrect routine at readable scale.
- Introduce the product before the corrective action begins.
- Show the process in causal order with one action per beat.
- Match the before and after camera angle, crop, lighting, subject, and surface.
- End with the product beside the result or the creator presenting the changed state.

## Camera And Sound

- Use stable close framing on the exact comparison area.
- Keep lighting consistent until the matched result is captured.
- Use spray, scrub, wipe, brush, pour, or handling sounds to reinforce the process.

## Proof And Continuity

- Require visible before, product action, intermediate process, and matched after.
- Lock identity, problem intensity, product amount, action order, surface geometry, and light direction.

## Avoid

- Unmatched before/after framing or lighting.
- Jumping directly to a result that implies an unsupported transformation.
- Treating a reaction shot as the only result evidence.

## Generator Risks

Identity consistency, exact surface matching, progressive state change, liquid or foam behavior, hair continuity, hands, and accidental over-transformation.
